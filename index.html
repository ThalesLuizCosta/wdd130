Santos

Santos is a municipality in the state of São Paulo in Brazil. It is the seat of the Metropolitan Region of Baixada Santista and has the largest economic participation in the aforementioned region. It is home to the largest port in Latin America, which is primarily responsible for the city's economic dynamics alongside tourism, fishing, and commerce. It ranks 5th among the most important non-capitals for the Brazilian economy and 10th according to quality of life.
